# Decision Log

Record decisions that are expensive to reverse, affect public or data contracts, alter scientific meaning, introduce material security or cost exposure, or constrain future implementation. Append new entries; supersede old decisions instead of rewriting history.

## Status Values

`proposed` | `accepted` | `superseded` | `rejected`

## Entry Template

```markdown
## ADR-NNN: Short Decision Title

- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Status: proposed
- Owners: names or roles
- Related: issue, plan, evaluation, or prior ADR

### Context
Facts, constraints, evidence, and forces that require a decision.

### Decision
The chosen direction and its precise scope.

### Alternatives
Viable options considered and why they were not chosen.

### Consequences
Benefits, drawbacks, scientific impact, security risk, cost, migration, and reversibility.

### Validation
Tests, evaluations, monitoring, and conditions that would trigger reconsideration.
```
